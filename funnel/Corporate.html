Corporate Accounts

SearchSystems.net offers additional discounts and billing options for businesses and professionals.

Please call our office at the number below if:

 

1. You would like to pay for an annual membership by check.

We can invoice you and set up your account once we receive payment. This type of membership will not include Premium searches, unless you would like to supply a credit card for those purchases. Please call us for details on monthly Premium search invoicing.

 

2. You would like to set up more than one user account.

We can send you an invoice for a single payment for multiple accounts. If you set up 3 or more accounts, the annual rate per user drops to $24.95. Please call for details.

 

3. You would like to be invoiced monthly for Premium searches.

If your business expects to perform more than 30 searches per month, we can invoice you monthly instead of requiring a credit card payment for each search. Please call for details.

 

4. You would like to be invoiced monthly for Flat Rate plans.

We can invoice you and set up your account once we receive payment. Please call for details.

 

5. You live outside of the United States and wish to perform Find People and/or Flat Rate searches.

We are not set up for credit card payments from customers outside of the U.S. for the Find People searches. We can, however, accept payment by check. Please call for details.
