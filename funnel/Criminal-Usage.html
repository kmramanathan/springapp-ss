More information about SearchSystems.net Criminal Records

Don’t have a SearchSystems.net membership?

Non-Members do not have to register as members to use our service.  They may do up to three free searches through our public records directory, and access our Premium Criminal Records database at a cost of $19.95 per nationwide search or $9.95 statewide.

 

Membership is $19.95 per year.  If you choose to become a member, you get unlimited access to the 47,000 databases in our public records directory, which means that you have full access as well to over 3,500 links to criminal, inmate, want & warrant, and sex offender databases at the local, county, state, national, and International levels, allowing you to access information in jurisdictions not covered by any commercial nationwide search.

 

Membership also gives you discounts on our Premium databases, so if you’d like to access our Premium Criminal Records database the cost is $14.95 per nationwide search or $6.95 per statewide search.

 

If you choose a Flat Rate Criminal Records plan, you can do up to 100 searches per day each day your plan is active.  All our Flat Rate Plans include SearchSystems.net membership, so whether you choose a one-day, week, month, or one-year plan, you get unlimited access to our public records directory AND access to and discounts on all of our Premium databases. So if you choose a Flat Rate plan, you get access to all three resources: Flat Rate criminal record searches, Premium criminal record searches, and our public records directory.

 

Information for SearchSystems.net Members

Your membership gives you access to and discounts on our Premium databases, and unlimited access to our Public Records Directory where you can dig deep to get court dockets and documents, court calendars, arrest records, wants and warrants, and get direct access to jurisdictions not covered by any commercial nationwide search.

  

The advantage of your Premium Criminal Records database access is that you can do partial name searches on first and last name, search by age range, access records that have only a partial or no date of birth, and get more detailed records.

 

If you choose to add a Flat Rate Criminal Records plan, you can do up to 100 searches per day each day your plan is active.  You are charged only for the plan, not each search.  The advantages of the Flat Rate database are that it gives you the freedom to explore name and birth date variations and gives you valuable information without having to worry about the cost of every search.
