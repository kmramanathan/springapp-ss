Looking for Criminal Records?

Two Huge Databases! Two ways to search!

SearchSystems.net provides the most comprehensive resource on the Internet for criminal records and background checks with two ways to search:

1.

Criminal Records Directory

Our Public Records Directory includes over 3,500 links to criminal, inmate, want & warrant, and sex offender databases at the local, county, state, national, and International levels. These links go straight to the sources, allowing you to access information in jurisdictions not covered by the nationwide searches. Many sources offer free access to docket sheets, case files, court documents and calendars, arrest records, and photographs. If you know the county where your subject has a criminal record, you can look here first instead of paying for a statewide search. SearchSystems.net members get unlimited access. Non-members get 1 free search.

 

2.

Premium Criminal Record Searches

Our pay-per-search Premium database now contains over 350 million records and provides powerful search features, such as partial names, middle initial, age range, and records that contain no date or only a partial date of birth. Search nationwide or by state and receive robust, detailed results. Includes thousands of local, county, state, national, and International sources, including terrorism, sanctions, and enforcement databases. Members pay $14.95 for each nationwide search and $6.95 for each statewide search.
